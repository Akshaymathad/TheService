Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a CMS?

Not in the traditional sense. We are a headless content engine designed for developers. You push raw data (JSON, HTML) and we serve it. No WYSIWYG editors, just pure API power.

How fast is the propagation?

Global propagation typically takes less than 150ms. We use a distributed edge network to ensure your content is always fresh.

Can I host images?

Yes, we support static asset hosting including images, fonts, and PDFs, served via our global CDN.
